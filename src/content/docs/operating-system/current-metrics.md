Internal rule of thumb: when average door count of demos drops below ~300, the list is approaching saturation. **SFR is at or below this threshold.** The existing SFR outbound list is largely exhausted.

## What we don't measure (and should)

The team openly acknowledges this gap. There is currently no:

- **Funnel-to-funnel conversion rates by stage.** No clean view of demo → opp → won → activated → live → churn rates.
- **Channel-specific CAC.** Cost per lead, cost per demo, cost per won deal, cost per activated account, by channel.
- **LTV cohorted by segment.** SFR vs. GS vs. integration-acquired vs. inbound. Each has different unit economics.
- **Month-over-month channel performance.** A standing dashboard surfacing which channels are improving, flat, or declining.
- **Diminishing-returns measurement.** The 10% close rate drop is one data point, not a system.
- **Marketing-attributed pipeline vs. BDR-sourced.** What share of pipeline marketing actually influences.
- **Cost-per-MQL by channel.** Direct mail, cold email, paid search, conferences, influencer, partner.
- **Expansion and churn cohorted by acquisition channel and segment.**
- **Time-to-revenue by integration.** How quickly a newly signed PM moves from contract to billing.
