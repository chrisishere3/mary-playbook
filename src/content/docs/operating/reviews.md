## How to use this template

- **Run it at the end of every quarter.** First Friday of the new quarter is a reasonable cadence.
- **Be specific.** "Email outperformed" isn't useful. "LOW-RISE campaign 40% close rate vs 20% on LOW-RISE ONSITE — the audience matters more than the copy" is useful.
- **Don't sanitize the "didn't work" section.** That's the section that compounds. The "what worked" section is for sharing publicly. The "didn't work" section is for getting smarter.
- **Use the frameworks.** Pull numbers using the [Frameworks](/mary-playbook/operating/frameworks/) page formulas. Use the same units across quarters so they're comparable.

## Where there's more room

- **Mid-quarter check-in.** A 30-min review at week 6 of each quarter would catch drift early. Today there's no formal mid-quarter cadence.
- **A scorecard with green / yellow / red status.** Helpful for Ben at a glance. Visual.
- **YoY comparison.** Once we have 4 quarters of these, the trend matters more than the absolute number.
