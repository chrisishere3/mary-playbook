## Channels we don't run yet

- **SEO / content.** Organic produces deals but attribution is messy. No content engine.
- **Influencer marketing.** Peter Lohmann was a one-off win. No systematic program.
- **PR.** Zero owned. The TenantCloud win is an obvious story we haven't told publicly.
- **Webinars / virtual events.** Untested.
- **Reseller / agency channel.** A few PM consultants exist who could resell. Untested.

## Channel grading principle

From the operating system: **goal is most doors for the cheapest price.** Watch for channels that drive demos but don't convert to activation. Always look at the activated stage, not just demos.

Today we track cost per MQL well and cost per activated door inconsistently. That's a measurement gap, not a strategy gap — see [How We Measure](/mary-playbook/playbook/how-we-measure/).
